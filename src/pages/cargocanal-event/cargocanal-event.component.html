CargoCanal has officially launched on October 25, 2018

CargoCanal is a platform that allows to manage and improve the logistics service of the country by facilitating the information exchange between maritime, freight forwarders, consignees and other stakeholders in the freight forwarding industry. It is also designed to help other stakeholders like national banks, customs and ministry of transportation to addess the issues they experience relating to import and export. This platform has been launched on 25th of december at UNECA with guests invited from different stakeholders of the industry.

As mentioned in the ceremony "Ethiopia is 170th among 176 countries in the quality of logistics service." one of the main reasons for this is lack of fast and real time information exchange between stakeholders and actors of the industry.CargoCanal shows the location and status of each cargos,it hardly believed that it will solve many problems in the industry.

on the launching ceremony the director of Ethiopian Maritime Affairs Authority(EMMA) director Mr. Mekonen Abera said "logistics without information is impossible.The platform we are launching today is going to digitalize and modernize the logistcs service".
Mr. Alemayehu Tolosa, one of the investors in the industry said " we were not be able to trace our cargo and it was very hard to get our cargo after it entered to one of the dry ports ,but now we can get such kind of information at real time".

The cermony of the launching includes program announcement, welcome messages, opening remark, keynote speech ,CargoCanal presentation, discussion on the presentation and official launching and declaration of CargoCanal followed by lunch program.
